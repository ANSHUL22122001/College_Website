Library

Chandigarh University has 13 libraries - one central library and twelve libraries situated in different academic blocks with a rich collection of more than 85,000 books and 850000 of e-books. It has an institutional membership with DELNET and British Council Library.

Cafeteria

Conveniently located cafes and mini refreshment joints are popular gathering places for students, faculty, and staff to eat, study, or socialize with friends.Restaurants and cafe's operational on campus are Lord's Food Court,Cafe Coffee Day, Baskin &Robbins and Subway.

Hostel

Chandigarh University campus has separate hostels for boys and girls with an attached mess. All the hostels have basic amenities like water purifiers, common room, gym, salon, recreation room, mess, TV, laundry services, security, and surveillance system.
